# Reading Momentum Vision

Reading Momentum is a social reading accountability web app that helps people build a consistent reading habit with friends while allowing every person to read their own book, format, goal, and pace.

The product is not a replacement for Goodreads, traditional book clubs, or personal reading trackers. Its job is to help users keep going when reading would otherwise lose momentum.

## Core Idea

Users read what they want, log progress in a low-pressure way, answer thoughtful prompts, interact with friends, earn balanced rewards, and gradually build a visible reading identity.

## Product Goal

Reading Momentum should help users become the type of person who reads consistently.

The product succeeds when it can clearly answer yes to this question:

> Did this app help someone keep reading when they otherwise would have stopped?

## Target Users

Initial users:

- College students
- Young professionals
- Friend groups
- Remote friend groups
- Casual readers
- People trying to reduce passive screen time
- People who like self-improvement apps
- People who enjoy books but dislike formal book clubs

Later expansion may include schools, libraries, professional learning groups, corporate book clubs, creator-led communities, and public reading circles.

## First-Version Focus

The first version should stay focused on small private groups. Trust and comfort matter because users are more likely to share progress and reflections when they know the people seeing them.
