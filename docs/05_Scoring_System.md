# Scoring System

Reading Momentum uses three separate progression systems so one score does not try to do everything.

## Systems

- Reading Momentum: current consistency and engagement
- XP: action rewards and cosmetic or profile unlocks
- Mastery: long-term accomplishments

## Reading Momentum

Reading Momentum is the main weekly score.

It should reward:

- Reading consistency
- Recent check-ins
- Discussion participation
- Reflection quality
- Reading volume with diminishing returns
- Comeback behavior after missed days

It should not reward unlimited page volume in a linear way.

Suggested pillars:

- Consistency: 40%
- Volume: 20%
- Reflection: 20%
- Community: 10%
- Completion: 10%

## XP

XP should reward positive actions such as reading check-ins, discussion responses, replies to friends, book completion, review submission, book nomination, streak milestones, and weekly comeback.

XP may unlock profile themes, badges, achievement displays, bookshelf customization, and group titles.

## Achievements

Achievements should recognize different reading identities.

Examples:

- First Check-In
- First Book Finished
- 7-Day Reader
- Weekend Reader
- Discussion Starter
- Supportive Friend
- Deep Reader
- Audiobook Listener
- Genre Explorer
- Review Writer
- Comeback Reader

## Fair Leaderboards

Recommended categories:

- Most Consistent
- Most Reflective
- Most Supportive
- Most Improved
- Most Books Finished
- Longest Current Streak
- Best Comeback
- Most Active Discussion

Avoid making total pages the main leaderboard.
