# Research Basis

Reading Momentum is grounded in research themes from habit formation, motivation theory, digital behavior change, and reading behavior.

## Research Themes

- People are more likely to maintain behaviors when goals are small and clear.
- Self-monitoring helps users recognize progress.
- Prompts and cues help users return to the behavior.
- Autonomy supports motivation.
- Relatedness supports motivation.
- Positive reinforcement helps habit formation.
- Social accountability can improve engagement when it feels supportive.
- Gamification can improve return behavior when it supports the core activity.
- Reading apps should avoid rewarding only raw quantity because reading quality and format differ widely.

## Documentation Standard

Major feature decisions should eventually include:

- Research source
- Design implication
- Risk
- Success metric
